Step-by-Step Math Guides

Addition

Formula: a + b = sum
  1. Line up the numbers vertically.
  2. Add each column from right to left.
  3. Carry over if necessary.

Example: 456 + 789

			   456
			+ 789
			------
			  1245

Quick Trick:

Break numbers into parts: 456 + (700 + 89) = 1156 + 89 = 1245

Subtraction

Formula: a - b = difference
  1. Line up the numbers.
  2. Subtract each column from right to left.
  3. Borrow if needed.

Example: 923 - 478

			   923
			- 478
			------
			   445

Quick Trick:

Use rounding: Instead of subtracting 478, subtract 500 (-22), then add 22 back.

Multiplication

Formula: a × b = product
  1. Multiply the bottom number by each digit of the top number.
  2. Shift left for each new row.
  3. Add all partial results.

Example: 23 × 12

			   23
			× 12
			------
			   46
			+ 230
			------
			  276

Quick Trick:

Use distributive property: 23 × 12 = (23 × 10) + (23 × 2) = 230 + 46 = 276

Division

Formula: a ÷ b = quotient
  1. Divide the first number of the dividend.
  2. Multiply and subtract.
  3. Bring down the next digit and repeat.

Example: 96 ÷ 4

			   96 ÷ 4 = 24

Quick Trick:

Split into halves: 96 ÷ 4 = (96 ÷ 2) ÷ 2 = 48 ÷ 2 = 24

Percentages

Formula: (part ÷ whole) × 100 = %

Example: What is 25% of 120?

			   120 × 0.25 = 30

Quick Trick:

To find 25%, divide by 4 (120 ÷ 4 = 30).

Fractions

Formula: a/b + c/d = (ad + bc) / bd

Example: 1/2 + 1/3

			   (1×3 + 1×2) / (2×3) = 5/6

Quick Trick:

Use LCM to simplify common denominators.

Math Hacks

1. Multiplying by 5

Instead of multiplying by 5, multiply by 10 and divide by 2.

Example: 24 × 5 → (24 × 10) ÷ 2 = 240 ÷ 2 = 120

2. Squaring a Number Ending in 5

Take the first digit, multiply it by the next number, then add 25.

Example: 35² → (3×4)25 = 1225

3. Divisibility Rules

  • By 2: Last digit is even.
  • By 3: Sum of digits is divisible by 3.
  • By 5: Last digit is 0 or 5.
